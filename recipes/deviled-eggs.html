Deviled Eggs

Description

Deviled eggs are an appetizer or side dish made from hard-boiled eggs that are peeled, cut in half, and filled with a creamy paste of their own yolks mixed with other ingredients like mayonnaise, mustard, and relish. They are a popular party food, often garnished with paprika or other seasonings.

Ingredients

  • 6 boiled eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • Prepared mustard or homemade yellow mustard
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Salt
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Peel hard-boiled eggs.
  2. Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks; set whites aside.
  3. Mash yolks with a fork in a small bowl. Stir in mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, onion, and celery; mix well and season with salt to taste.
  4. Stuff or pipe egg yolk mixture into egg whites.
  5. Sprinkle with paprika. Refrigerate eggs until serving.
